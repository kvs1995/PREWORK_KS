Biography

Dominic Fike was born December 30th, 1995 in Naples, Florida. Fike has a 2 brothers and 1 sister and is of Filipino and African-American descent.

He initially started making beats and releasing songs on SoundCloud, all of which gathered a large following. He released his first EP Don't Forget About Me, Demos in December 2017, when he was only 21 years old. This EP was released when he was on house arrest for battery of a police officer. The EP started a bidding war to sign Fike. The Columbia label ended up signing Dominic for approximately $4 million right before serving time in jail for violating his house arrest. He would later go on to collaborate with band Brockhampton and their leader Kevin Abstract, a clothing collaboration with Marc Jacobs, release a second album called What Could Possibly Go Wrong and become the subject of an episode of The New York Times Presnet on Hulu. Most recently, Fike announced that he has joined the cast for the second season of Euphoria on HBO Max.
